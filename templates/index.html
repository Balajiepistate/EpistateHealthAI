Epident AI - Treatment Plan





What EpistateHealth AI Can Help With

  • Visual Detection: Identifies potential anomalies such as cavities, bone density loss, impacted teeth, periapical radiolucency, and restoration issues.
  • Structural Description: Analyzes and labels dental anatomy: molars, incisors, crowns, implants, fillings, bridges, etc.
  • Preliminary Treatment Suggestions: Offers suggestions such as scaling, restoration, root canal therapy, extraction, or periodontal follow-up — based on the visual evidence.
  • Comparative Review: Assists in comparing before-and-after images to assess treatment effectiveness over time.
  • Patient Communication Support: Generates clear, layperson-friendly summaries and visual references to help dentists educate patients.
  • Workflow Support: Accelerates case discussions, treatment planning, and second-opinion generation — especially in high-volume practices.

Note to Users

EpistateHealth AI is designed as a clinical support tool to enhance dental professionals' workflows. It performs best with high-quality, well-lit oral images or clear panoramic/periapical X-rays. Interpretations and treatment suggestions are probabilistic in nature, based on general datasets. Clinical decisions must always incorporate clinical judgment, patient history, and in-person examination. The tool is best used for preliminary assessment, educational explanation, and treatment planning discussions.
